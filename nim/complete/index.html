Rules

There are 16 matches, arranged in four rows. There are seven matches in the first row, five in the second row, three in the third row and one in the last row.

When it is your turn:

  • You can remove one or more matches from any row
  • You must remove at least one match
  • You can only remove matches from one row
  • The player who takes the last match is the loser.
